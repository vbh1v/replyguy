ReplyGuy
A minimal, beautiful extension to generate smart replies on Twitter/X using AI. No clutter, just results.
  • Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  • Open the extension and add your API key (OpenAI, DeepSeek, or OpenRouter).
  • Choose your preferred AI model.
  • Go to x.com and use the shortcut to generate a reply.
  • Click the generated reply to send it.
